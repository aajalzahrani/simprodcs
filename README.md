# Privacy Policy & Account Deletion Policy
**Last updated: April 2026**
InventoryPro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the InventoryPro mobile application.
This page explains how we collect, use, and manage your data, including how you can request account deletion.
---
## 1. Information We Collect
### Personal Information
* Email address (used for login and authentication)
### Usage Data
* App interactions
* Device information (non-sensitive)
---
## 2. How We Use Information
We use your data to:
* Provide and maintain the application
* Authenticate users securely
* Improve app performance and experience
---
## 3. Data Storage and Security
We use **Firebase (Google services)** to store and process data.
* Data is encrypted in transit
* Access is restricted to authenticated users only
---
## 4. Camera Usage
InventoryPro uses your device camera to:
* Scan barcodes and QR codes
We do NOT store or share any images or video captured by the camera.
---
## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data.
We only use trusted third-party services:
* Firebase Authentication
* Firebase Firestore
---
## 6. User Rights
You have the right to:
* Access your data
* Request deletion of your account and data
* Stop using the app at any time
---
# 🔴 7. Account Deletion Policy
Users can request deletion of their account and associated data.
### How to request deletion:
Send an email to:
📧 **[simprodcs@gmail.com](mailto:simprodcs@gmail.com)**
Include:
* Your registered email address
* Request subject: "Delete My Account"
---
### What happens after request:
* Your account will be deleted within **3–5 business days**
* All associated data will be permanently removed
* This action is irreversible
---
### Data that will be deleted:
* User account information (email)
* Inventory data associated with your account
---
### Data that may be retained:
* Minimal logs for security or legal purposes (if required)
---
